    @eCommerce-and-buisness

    eCommerce websites are websites where people use to directly buy products from. You have probably used many eCommerce websites before, most big brands and plenty of smaller ones have one. Any website that includes a shopping cart and a way for you to provide credit card information to make a purchase falls into this category. Tips:  Keep it simple  Make branding a priority  Think like a website visitor  Use colour to your advantage  Use high-quality images  Make your content scannable  Make it look professional  Make categories easy to navigate Business websites are websites that are devoted to representing a specific business. E-commerce websites are business websites however, business websites do not sell anything directly. For example, they encourage visitors to get in contact for more information. Tips:  Make your site mobile responsive  Make it easy to find  Make it easy to navigate  Keep your page organised  Make sure it is accurate

    @entertainment-and-media

    Entertainment websites are websites that people visit purely for entertainment purposes. They could be humours websites with fun and interesting content like Buzzfeed. Most of these websites, usually make money through advertisements that show up on their pages. If you want to start an entertainment website there are plenty of options, you can make funny or informative videos, write entertaining blog posts, draw comics, or create fun quizzes. Tips:  There are many entertainment websites out there, you should anticipate taking some time and work hard to find ideas that will connect you with the audience  Ensure it is mobile-friendly  Make time to plan  Cover the need-to-knows  Keep it simple Media websites are websites that collect news stories or other reporting. There is some overlap with the entertainment websites. However, media websites are more likely to include reported pieces in addition to or instead of content meant purely for entertainment. This category includes sites like the Washington Post website, Slate, and Inc. Tips:  Implement consistent page branding  Craft profile descriptions carefully Display reviews

    @educational

    Educational website are websites that have the primary goal of either providing educational materials to visitors or providing information on an educational institution to them. Educational websites can include websites that have games, videos or topic related resources that act as tools to enhance learning and supplement classroom teaching. These websites help make the process of learning entertaining and attractive to the student, especially in today's age. Tips:  Accuracy of Information  User-Friendly Navigation  Keep Your Educational Website Design Simple
